जल ही जीवन हे ! The country like India where rivers are worshipped as Mother of livelihoods suffering from major water problems. Carelessness about the water resources, negligence about water wastage and worldwide global warming is the root cause. According to Wild Water, State of the World’s Water 2017, 63.4 million–living without access to clean water in India. Clean water is the basis of health. Not only do the rural poor drink highly contaminated water, but many even have no awareness that water is the reason for illness and death. To date, there has been no access to reliably clean water at an affordable price and no efforts made to address and open up markets for the rural poor. Alt commerce in collaboration with Tejobindu Hindustan Foundation took small steps to help the people living in the remote village Bhanpur Chhapra of Madhya Pradesh (central India) by distributing the Gravity based water filters to enable them to have access to clean drinking water. We live now in an era wherein everything is made possible. The modernization and development of technologies and machines have skyrocketed throughout the years. This had left us all in awe, about how certain things come together and result in something greater than we cannot easily grasp or understand and leave us feeling surprised. One of the examples that can surprise us with its greatness is the Gravity Water Filter. Gravity based water purifiers are capable of efficiently removing coarse particles like sand, dirt, dust and microorganisms like bacteria, viruses, and parasites from the water. Gravity based water purifiers generally use fiber mesh, activated carbon filters, and some sort of polisher which removes leftover chemicals. The best advantage of gravity-based water filters is, it does not even require any electricity. In fact, they are better than boiled water. At the same time, the "Tejobindu Hindustan Foundation" organization provided knowledge about water and its management. How can we manage the rainwater? Is it possible to do rain harvesting in small areas? How rain harvesting will help in the future all the questions are answered by some of the live examples already done in some parts of India. The time is warning us to manage the environment properly. Otherwise, we all are specimen's in the near future. Come and join us. Together we will achieve a lot more. You can also donate for the cause now. All these steps can make a small but significant contribution to improving the environment.
